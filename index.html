Which Greek Tragic Figure Are You?

A small fate quiz inspired by characters from Classical Mythology (CLA204). Answer a few questions about how you make choices, and see which tragic figure your patterns resemble.

1. When a serious problem appears in your life, what is your first instinct?

2. How do you feel about “fate”?

3. Someone close to you betrays you. What do you do?

4. What kind of ending feels strangely “right” to you, even if it isn’t happy?

This project reimagines Greek tragic figures as a modern “fate quiz,” using interactive choices to highlight patterns of heroism, betrayal, and responsibility.
