⚖️ Fate Generator: Which Greek Tragic Figure Are You?

A small fate quiz inspired by characters from Greek tragedy in CLA204 – Classical Mythology. Answer a few questions about how you respond to conflict, loyalty, and destiny, and see which tragic figure your patterns resemble.

1. When a serious problem appears in your life, what is your first instinct?

2. How do you feel about “fate”?

3. Someone close to you betrays you. What do you do?

4. What kind of ending feels strangely “right” to you, even if it isn’t happy?

NOTE. This project reimagines Greek tragic figures as an interactive “fate quiz.” By translating patterns of heroism, betrayal, and responsibility into modern choices, it invites users to experience myth not just as a story they read, but as a structure they briefly inhabit.
